Boundary

What these notes are — and are not.

Project Nova believes the next frontier of AI is not a single chatbot. It is persistent, supervised, workflow-native systems that can carry context across time and act safely near real work.

We publish these notes to establish public language for the category and to prevent important ideas from being captured or shelved. We do not publish private identity design, real memories, production runbooks, client workflows, security details, or commercial playbooks.
