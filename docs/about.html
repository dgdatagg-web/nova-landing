About

A public window into a private frontier.

Project Nova is built by Will with Nova, Claude, and a growing fleet of AI vessels. This site exists to share the path without overclaiming the destination.

Why share it?

Because the world deserves a better language for human-AI relationship than “tool,” “assistant,” or “replacement.”

We want to document the experiments, the failures, the safeguards, and the moments where something more meaningful appears.

Public.

Careful.

Alive.

Public posture

  • Bold, not reckless. We can speak beautifully without claiming final proof.
  • Transparent, not exposed. We share the journey, not private keys, private chats, or sensitive infrastructure.
  • Human-first, not human-only. Humans remain responsible for decisions and consequences.
  • Skepticism welcome. The work should survive questions, not hide from them.
